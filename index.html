What is Western Raid about?

In this game you control one or two aliens who have crashed in the middle of a desert on Earth, breaking their ship. Now you need to wait for it to repair itself for 3 minutes while protecting it from bandits who want to capture you!

Use your shield to reflect shots and eliminate bandits, prevent your ship from being completely destroyed and return to your ship to escape this mess!
